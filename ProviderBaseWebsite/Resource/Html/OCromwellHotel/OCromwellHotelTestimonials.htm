...Superb Find
I stayed at the hotel for 2 nights in February 2012..From email enquiries to the actual booking, the staff couldn't have been more helpful. I needed a quiet room to work and that was exactly what I got. The room was extremely large with a balcony although I see most of the rooms do have balconies and it really helps in not feeling hemmed in as I often do in hotels. The bathroom was excellent and the raindrop shower by far the most powerful I have ever experienced. The towels were large and thick and a welcome change from the stamp like offerings which can prevail in other hotels. The toiletries were of excellent quality. The bed - very large and comfy. Nice sheets which where brilliant white and not too crispy.
Good free view TV and ample space for clothes, bags etc.... Complimentary water in the room, refreshed every day. Wifi - free throughout the hotel. I did find the signal a little weak at times but I was never without internet access.
The staff - now these are the jewels in the crown - friendly and welcoming and genuinely interested in the guests. The food - good, honest to goodness. Not fancy or fussy and plenty of it. Fresh eggs on request at breakfast. I will definitely return to the hotel when I am back in the area with work and wish I had found it years ago. I have stayed in some grimsville locations prior to this!
The hotel is very ‘green’ and has solar panels on the hotel.
Thank you for a lovely stay and being my home from home for 2 days. Just what the weary business traveler needs!
